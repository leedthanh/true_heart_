Heart disease is a number one cause of death in the USA. True-Heart can predicts your risk of heart disease

Machine learning model TRUE-HEART was trained on data from the CDC surveyed in 2020.
The dataset contains more than 300,000 rows and 18 columns with different indicators.

BidiBomBom is a collaborative class project with Md Yousuf Reja and Maria Parra from Rice University.
We used a smaller dataset for our project and less indicators mostly ECG and blood tests to predict heart disease.
