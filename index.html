Heart disease is a number one cause of death in the USA. True-Heart can predicts your risk of heart disease

Machine learning model TRUE-HEART was trained on data from the CDC surveyed in 2020.
The dataset contains more than 300,000 rows and 18 columns with different indicators of heart disease.

TRUE-HEART inspired by a collaborative class project with Md Yousuf Reja and Maria Parra from Rice University. We used a smaller dataset for that project and less indicators mostly ECG and blood tests to predict heart failure. The BidiBomBom model was designed for medical professionals usage only. However if you want to try you can also find it here.


Behold TRUE-HEART: the model that can help you discover heart disease.

Technology and healthcare have joined forces to bring you True-Heart, a cutting-edge model designed to assist in the early detection of heart disease. While True-Heart is a powerful tool, it's important to note that it is not a replacement for professional medical advice. Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized guidance.
